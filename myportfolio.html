MY PORTFOLIO

charminar

The Charminar is a wondrous monument and mosque in Old City of Hyderabad region, in the state of Telangana, India. The Mosque is on the second floor of this structure. It was build in 1591 CE. It is one of the most recognized structures of this nation. This particular landmark is the iconic attraction of Hyderabad, visited by huge number of tourists from across India and abroad.

Golconda Fort

The Golkonda Fort is one of the popular ruined forts of Southern part of India largely visited by domestic and foreign tourists. During 1518 to 1687, Golkonda was the capital of medieval Golconda Sultanate. It is located just 11 km west of Hyderabad, in the state of Telungana, India. This particular region is worldwide popular for the mines that have largely produced the world's most coveted and famous gems such as the Darya-i-Noor, the Koh-i-Noor, the Nassak Diamond and the Hope Diamond.

salar jung museum

Salar Jung Museum is put up at Salar Jung Road, Darulshifa Block, Hyderabad District of Telangana State in India. It is the class museum of tourist importance for its vast collection of antiquities dated back to 4th Century BC to the latest era. This museum was established in 1951 AD and named it in honor of its founder Salar Jung. It is a very excellent place to fine arts personals, students, antique lovers, historians and scholars.

mecca masjid

Mecca Masjid is the most superannuated mosque in Hyderabad built in 1694. It is the largest mosque to accommodate 10,000 peoples in a sitting prayer at a time. It is an unparalleled Islamic structure in India. This Masjid is listed in the heritage monuments in India. It is located in the south central part of the Deccan plate. To reach here one must come to Hyderabad presently the capital of Telengana and previously Andhra Pradesh.

warangal fort

Warangal Fort is located in Killa, Mathwada block, Warangal District, Telengana State of South India. It is a lost treasure of Kakatiya Dynasty who ruled the Deccan Province in the 13th Century AD. Currently it won't look like a fort with large walls and cannons. Since, it was attacked by the Khilgi dynasty of North India and what are seen here are the remains of vast man made destruction. Still its large arches and few magnificent structures remain strong enough to withstand in time.

surendrapuri theme park

Surendrapuri Theme Park is a famous place of Hindu Mythological Crafts work. It is located in Yadagiri Gutta, Nallgonda District of Telengana State in India. It is around 55 KM from Hyderabad City. Tourist come here to see the life size sculptures and temples carved in granite stones, which are very unique, beautiful and color full to see. The theme of the park is Hindu Mythology. This museum is named as Kunda Satyanarayana Kala Dhamam.
